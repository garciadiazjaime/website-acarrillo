Andrea Carrillo Iglesias (Mexico, 1986) is an artist and designer living and working between Tijuana, Mexico City and Los Angeles. Carrillo holds a BA in Graphic Design from Art Center College of Design, and a Masters degree in Art, Design and the Public Domain from Harvard University Graduate School of Design. In 2015 she coursed a year of Graduate Studies in Visual Arts at Sandberg Instituut, Rietveld Academie, Amsterdam.
